TipKey Information

All URLs included are official government or government-authorized portals verified as of 2025. Application timelines differ by country and program, but most require submission 3–12 months before the intended start date. Many destinations accept standard English proficiency tests such as IELTS (6.0–7.0) or TOEFL iBT (80–100). Scholarship deadlines often fall earlier than general admission deadlines, so applicants should plan accordingly. Most developed countries also offer post-study work permits lasting 1–4 years, depending on local immigration policies. Because requirements can change, applicants should always verify the most current information directly on the official portals before applying.
