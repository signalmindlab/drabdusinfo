Journal Templates

Academic Journal LaTeX Templates

Author

Dr. Md Abdus Samad

Published

May 30, 2025

This comprehensive guide provides LaTeX templates for major academic journals across various disciplines. Each template is designed to meet specific journal formatting requirements and ensure smooth submission processes.

WarningNote

For some templates, direct links might not work due to security issues or URL changes. In that case, browse from the publisher’s homepage or use Overleaf templates.

TipTips for Using Templates
  • Always download the latest version of templates from official sources
  • Check journal-specific requirements before submission
  • Use Overleaf for collaborative editing and automatic compilation
  • Keep a backup of your original manuscript before applying templates
  • Verify bibliography style requirements for each journal
  • When official links fail, Overleaf templates are usually up-to-date alternatives
