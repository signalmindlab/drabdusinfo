Document Conversion and Academic Figure Tools

Transform Your Academic Workflow with Pandoc and Professional Illustration Tools

Author

Dr. Md Abdus Samad

Published

May 30, 2025

Transform your academic workflow with Pandoc — a powerful, free, and open-source document converter that handles Word, LaTeX, PDF, Markdown, HTML, EPUB, and more. Perfect for researchers and students who need seamless format conversion with citation support.

Essential Pandoc Commands

Word to PDF Conversion

pandoc myfile.docx -o myfile.pdf --pdf-engine=xelatex

Markdown to Word

pandoc myfile.md -o myfile.docx

LaTeX to Word

pandoc myfile.tex -o myfile.docx

Word to LaTeX

pandoc myfile.docx -o myfile.tex

Markdown to PDF

pandoc myfile.md -o myfile.pdf --pdf-engine=xelatex

HTML to Word

pandoc myfile.html -o myfile.docx

Word to HTML

pandoc myfile.docx -o myfile.html

With Bibliography (Citations)

pandoc myfile.md -o myfile.pdf --bibliography=refs.bib --csl=apa.csl

Multiple Input Files

pandoc chapter1.md chapter2.md chapter3.md -o thesis.pdf

Extract Text from PDF

pandoc myfile.pdf -o myfile.txt

Advanced Pandoc Options

Custom PDF Styling

pandoc myfile.md -o myfile.pdf --pdf-engine=xelatex --template=custom.tex

Table of Contents

pandoc myfile.md -o myfile.pdf --toc --toc-depth=3

Numbered Sections

pandoc myfile.md -o myfile.pdf --number-sections

Extract Media from Word

pandoc myfile.docx --extract-media=./media -o myfile.md

Standalone HTML

pandoc myfile.md -o myfile.html --standalone --self-contained
NotePro Tip

Combine multiple options for complex conversions:

pandoc myfile.md -o myfile.pdf \
  --pdf-engine=xelatex \
  --bibliography=refs.bib \
  --csl=ieee.csl \
  --toc \
  --number-sections
